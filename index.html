What Is Project Finance?

Project Finance Intro, Nov 3, 2024

Project finance funds long-term infrastructure, industrial projects, and public services using a nonrecourse or limited-recourse financial structure. The debt and equity used to finance the project are repaid solely from the cash flow generated by the project itself.

Financial Markets

Financial Markets Intro, Nov 2, 2024

Financial Markets include any place or system that provides buyers and sellers the means to trade financial instruments, including bonds, equities, the various international currencies, and derivatives. Financial markets facilitate the interaction between those who need capital with those who have capital to invest. In addition to making it possible to raise capital, financial markets allow participants to transfer risk (generally through derivatives) and promote commerce.

What is FinTech?

FinTech Intro, Nov 1, 2024

FinTech, short for Financial Technology, is the integration of modern technology into financial services to improve their use and delivery.

One standout example of FinTech is mobile banking. With just a few taps on a smartphone, users can check their balance, pay bills, transfer money, and even deposit checks.
